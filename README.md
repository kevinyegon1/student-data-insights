Through initial data exploration, several clear trends emerged:

Skill Gap: The majority of students are beginners, with a significant drop-off in intermediate and advanced learners.

Low Engagement: Most students do not utilize Kaggle and have completed fewer than 4 projects.

Performance: The modal score range sits between 40-60 marks, leading to a high initial failure rate across the cohort.

Effort Distribution: Most students commit 5-8 hours per week, solving roughly 55-65 problems.
